What We Offer

Ever wonder what it means to become an official Ignition Hackathon?
Here's a list of perks we offer to official events during the Season!




Expert Mentorship

Tap into the Ignition & our partner networks' expert advice and mentorship. We'll sit in on your weekly organizer meetings to help.

Promotion

Your event will be posted on our website where thousands of high school hackers come and find events.

Blog Coverage

We take care of blog coverage for the event. We might help you set up a live stream of the hack demos at the end.



Prizes

We offer special High School prizes at major college hackathons. These include hardware, internships, etc.

Sponsors Hookup

Events are offered special MLH contacts to companies friendly towards sponsoring hackathons.

Branding Help

Don't have a logo or brand for your hackathon yet? Our designer will help you create one.



Bulk Discounts*

We've negotiated discounts on things like chips and stickers to help you save money.

Free Domain Names

Every hacker who participates in an Ignition event gets a free domain name thanks to Namecheap!

Free SMS & Emails

Send SMS updates and both Email notifications and newsletter to your attendees free of charge.



Mini-Events*

We'll organize a fun mini-event like an arcade or photobooth during your hackathon.

Hardware Lab*

We will bring the latest and greatest technology for your attendees to hack on.

Website Help*

Don't have a website for your hackathon yet? Our talented webdev will help you create one.

* Depending on region, demand, and availability

What We Require

To become an official hackathon, just complete these three simple steps!



1

High School Admittance

We require that your event is either a high school only hackathon or a MLH sanctioned college hackathon that accepts high scool students.

2

Sanction Guide

All hackathons are required to adhere to our sanctioning guide. These rules help assure that your event will be successful!

3

Branding

We require events to follow our branding guildlines. Rules include logo placement on website, etc.
