Useful Resources for hackathon organizers

What We Offer

Ever wonder what it means to become an official Minor League Hacking Hackathon?
Here's a list of perks we offer to official events during the Season!




Expert Mentorship

Tap into the MiLH & our partner networks' expert advice and mentorship. We'll sit in on your weekly organizer meetings to help.

Promotion

Your event will be posted on our website where thousands of high school hackers come and find events.

Blog Coverage

We take care of blog coverage for the event. We might help you set up a live stream of the hack demos at the end.



Prizes

We offer special High School prizes at major college hackathons. These include hardware, internships, etc.

Sponsors Hookup

Events are offered special MLH contacts to companies friendly towards sponsoring hackathons.

Mini-Events

We'll organize a fun mini-event like an Ice Cream Truck or Photobooth during your hackathon.



Bulk Discounts

We've negotiated discounts on things like hardware and stickers to help you save money.

Free Domain Names

Every hacker who participates in an MiLH event gets a free domain name thanks to Namecheap!

Free SMS & Emails

Send SMS updates and both Email notifications and newsletter to your attendees free of charge.



Stickers

Every event gets packs of stickers from our partners!

Branding Help

Don't have a logo or brand for your hackathon yet? Our designer will help your create one.

Website Help*

Don't have a website for your hackathon yet? Our talented webdev will help your create one.
*Depending on availability




What We Require



1

High School Admittance

We require that your event is either a high school only hackathon or a MLH sanctioned college hackathon that accepts high scool students.

2

Sanction Guide

All hackathons are required to adhere to our sanctioning guide. These rules help assure that your event will be successful!

3

Branding

We require events to follow our branding guildlines. Rules include logo placement on website, etc.
