What is Minor League Hacking?


Minor League Hacking is the definative high school hackathon league. We are an subset of Major League Hacking, the official college hackathon league, with a focus on High School students interested in computer science and programming. We aim to inspire students to build cool products and change the world through hackathons.

You may be interested in reading about hackathons and high school hackers.


Thinking of organizing a hackathon admitting high schoolers? We would love to get in touch with you!

Managed by the fine folks at MLH and @lablayers plus @jimmyliu.
