What is Minor League Hacking?


We are an subset of Major League Hacking, the official college hackathon league designed for High School students interested in computer science. We aim to inspire students to build cool products and change the world through hackathons.

You may be interested in reading about hackathons and high school hackers for more information.


Thinking of organizing a hackathon admitting high schoolers? We would love to get in touch with you!
